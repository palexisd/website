Signature management tools like Exclaimer are excellent for handling company-wide email signatures, but they can be prohibitively expensive. This guide will walk you through setting up your own signature management system for Gmail using primarily free tools—aside from the cost of a Google Workspace subscription. The objective is to automate the creation of email signatures for all users, streamlining the onboarding process and minimizing the risk of human error.

Configuring your template.s

Your templates will need to be created in HTML format. If you're not familiar with HTML, don’t worry! You can use AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to help you write the code. One of the great features of HTML signatures is the ability to embed links within images—such as linking your company’s website to your logo.
